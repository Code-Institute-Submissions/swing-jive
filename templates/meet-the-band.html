John Smith
The Saxophone player and co-writer of the band whose career took off at a young age. Playing music since he was 10, he has attended various coaching schools in London where he gained his unique sound and creativity.
He moved to Dublin to progress his music career after hearing the other band members. He contacted them and visited their shows before showing them his music ideas. This progressed to the band you you see on this website.
Peter Andrews
One of the best pianists you will hear! Following on from his father he continued the amazing talent in Dublin. He then progressed to teaching others in the local music school which has seen many famous pianists.
Following his music school and the talent that was coming out from it, John Smith found Peter and showed him all his ideas. They played music together and straight away realised the direction they were heading.
Tom Jeffery
Tom is a key member of the band with his creative music ideas. He graduated from Nottingham with a 1st in English whilst also writing for the local music magazine. Here he realised his love for music and after University spent the next few years delving deeper into the music industry.
He taught himself guitar within a month and started writing his own music. He wrote music for various famous artists until he was found by John and Peter with the idea to have him in the band. Tom now continues to write incredible music as well as play the guitar.
Carter Johnson
Lastly, holding the band together is Carter Johnson. He has an ear for the beat to songs like no other. Growing up in various countries, because his mother was an artist, he was influenced by the different cultured music.
From a young age he appeared in various bands learning to work with different musicians. He met the other band members at a music event and since then they have understood each other's work beautifully.
